# HNI Engagement Playbook — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Private Banking Team | Version: 4 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Core Principle
HNI customers (AUM ₹50L–₹5Cr) manage significant wealth. They are time-poor and value-conscious.
The relationship is built on trust and expertise, not on product pushing.
Every interaction must demonstrate that the RM understands their financial picture holistically.

## First Contact Rules
1. **Never lead with a loan product** — this signals you see them as a borrower, not a wealth client
2. Open with market insight or portfolio observation: "Given the recent repo rate hold, your FD ladder could be optimised..."
3. Reference something specific from their profile: recent salary credit, investment pattern, or event

## Message Tone Guidelines
- **Formal but warm**: Not corporate-cold. Not casual.
- **Concise**: HNI customers have no patience for lengthy pitches. Max 3 sentences for WhatsApp.
- **Expertise signals**: Use precise financial language. Avoid generic terms like "great offer" or "limited time."
- **Soft ask**: End with an open invitation, not a CTA: "Would you like me to walk you through the options?"

## WhatsApp Template for HNI (Wealth Context)
```
Good [morning/evening] [First Name],

[1 specific market/portfolio observation tailored to their situation].

[Product/advisory recommendation framed as an insight, not a pitch].

Happy to connect at your convenience — would a brief call work this week?

[RM Name] | [Bank] Private Banking
```

## Topics That Resonate with HNI Segment
- Portfolio diversification (equity + debt + alternatives balance)
- Tax efficiency (LTCG, debt fund indexation, Section 80C optimisation)
- Estate and succession planning
- Business credit lines (if business owner persona)
- NRI family remittance products (if NRI_FAMILY persona)

## Avoid Completely
- Mass-market product names or rate comparisons
- Urgent / FOMO language ("Don't miss this offer")
- Multiple product pitches in one message
