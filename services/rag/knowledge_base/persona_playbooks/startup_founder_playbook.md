# Startup Founder Playbook — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: SME Banking Team | Version: 2 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Profile Characteristics
Startup founders typically have irregular personal income (equity-heavy compensation),
significant business cash flows, and complex banking needs spanning personal and corporate.
They move fast and value speed over formality.

## Key Needs
- **Working capital**: Overdraft / credit line for operational expenses
- **Business current account**: High transaction limits, API banking, bulk payments
- **Personal loan**: Bridge finance during funding gap (if runway is tight)
- **Forex**: International payments, foreign investor receipts, SWIFT

## Message Tone
- **Direct and punchy**: Skip pleasantries. Get to the point in sentence 1.
- **Show you understand their world**: Reference GST, burn rate, runway, funding rounds.
- **Speed emphasis**: Emphasise quick approvals, digital process, no branch visits.
- **Respect their time**: Max 2 sentences for WhatsApp.

## WhatsApp Template (Business Credit)
```
Hi [First Name], noticed your business transaction volume has grown 40%+ — you may benefit from an enhanced credit line. Digital approval in 48 hours. Want details?
```

## Conversion Signals Specific to Startup Founders
- GST payment spike (business scaling)
- Multiple NEFT/IMPS to new beneficiaries (hiring or supplier expansion)
- Foreign inward remittances (investor payments)
- Salary credits from company to personal account started recently (founder paying themselves)
