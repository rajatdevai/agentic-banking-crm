# Young Professional Playbook — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Retail Banking Team | Version: 3 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Profile Characteristics
Age 22–35, salaried, likely in first or second job, building credit history.
Aspirational — wants quality products that signal status. Digital-first.
Responds to urgency and social proof. Open to cross-sell if trust is established.

## Key Needs
- **Personal loan**: Lifestyle (travel, wedding, electronics) or emergency bridge
- **Credit card upgrade**: From entry-level to Signature/Infinite tier
- **SIP/Mutual funds**: Starting wealth building journey
- **Home loan**: For first home purchase (often with parental co-applicant)

## Message Tone
- **Friendly and energetic**: Conversational. Use their first name prominently.
- **Aspirational framing**: Connect product to their life goals, not just financial utility.
- **Simple language**: Avoid jargon. Use plain language.
- **Clear CTA**: Young professionals appreciate direct asks — "Click here", "Reply YES".

## WhatsApp Template (Personal Loan)
```
Hey [First Name]! 🎉

[Personalized hook — e.g., "Planning a trip?" or "Getting married this season?"]

A personal loan of up to ₹[Amount] at [Rate]% is pre-approved for you — no paperwork, instant disbursal.

Tap to apply → [Link] | Reply STOP to opt out
```

## Conversion Signals
- Jewellery / wedding venue MCCs (wedding planning)
- Travel MCCs + international spends (vacation planning)
- EMI on electronics detected (upgrade appetite)
- Account balance below 10% of monthly salary for 3+ consecutive months (liquidity stress)
