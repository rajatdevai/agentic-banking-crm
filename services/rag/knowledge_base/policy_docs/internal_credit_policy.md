# Internal Credit Policy — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Credit Risk Department | Version: 12 | Effective: 2025-01-15
## Classification: INTERNAL — Not for external distribution

## Personal Loan Credit Thresholds
| CIBIL Score | Decision | Max Loan Amount |
|-------------|----------|-----------------|
| 750+ | Auto-approve (standard rate) | Up to ₹25 lakh |
| 700–749 | Approve with standard review | Up to ₹15 lakh |
| 650–699 | Manual review required; higher rate | Up to ₹5 lakh |
| Below 650 | Auto-decline | — |

## Risk Flag Definitions
- **HIGH_RISK**: 2+ missed EMIs in last 12 months OR CIBIL < 650
- **MONITOR**: 1 missed EMI in last 6 months OR CIBIL 650–679
- **CLEAR**: No delinquencies, CIBIL 700+

## Concentration Limits
- No single customer can hold > ₹1 crore in unsecured retail loans
- Total retail unsecured book not to exceed 15% of total advances

## Mandatory RM Review Conditions
- Any loan > ₹25 lakh requires RM sign-off
- Any customer with existing settlement history requires credit head approval
- Any self-employed customer with < 3 years ITR requires manual underwriting

## Negative List Employers (do not lend)
- Companies under IBC proceedings
- Employers with salary bounce rate > 10% in bank's portfolio
