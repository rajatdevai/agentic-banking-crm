# RBI Lending Guidelines Summary — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: RBI Master Circular | Version: 2025 | Effective: 2025-04-01

## Fair Practices Code
- All loan terms must be clearly communicated in writing before acceptance
- Interest rate must be expressed as APR (Annual Percentage Rate)
- No loan product can be bundled with insurance without explicit written consent

## Responsible Lending
- Lenders must assess borrower's repayment capacity before disbursement
- FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) must not exceed 55% for retail loans
- Negative amortisation is prohibited

## Digital Lending Guidelines (2023)
- All digital loan disbursements must go directly to borrower's bank account (no third-party)
- Cooling-off period: 3 days for loans above ₹10,000 (borrower can cancel without penalty)
- Key Fact Statement (KFS) must be provided before execution

## Data Privacy
- Customer data cannot be used for cross-sell without explicit consent
- Do Not Disturb (DND) registry must be checked before any outreach
- Customer has right to opt out of marketing communications at any time

## Outreach Compliance Rules
- Maximum 3 outreach attempts per product per customer per month
- Cannot contact between 9 PM and 9 AM
- All outreach must include opt-out instructions
