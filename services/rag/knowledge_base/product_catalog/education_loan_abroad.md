# Education Loan — Overseas Study Guide — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Product Catalog | Version: 1.4 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Overview
Education loans for overseas universities (USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany).
Covers tuition, living expenses, travel, and course materials.

## Eligibility
- Student age: 18–35 years
- Co-applicant required: parent or guardian (income-earning)
- University: Must be on approved list (top 500 QS World Rankings)
- CIBIL (co-applicant): 700+
- Admission letter: Mandatory before disbursement

## Loan Parameters
| Parameter | Range |
|-----------|-------|
| Loan amount | Up to ₹1.5 crore (USA/UK); ₹75 lakh (other) |
| Tenure | Up to 15 years (including moratorium) |
| Moratorium | Course duration + 12 months |
| Interest rate | 10% – 13.5% p.a. |
| Collateral | Required above ₹40 lakh |

## Conversion Signals
- SWIFT/international wire transfers to education institutions
- Visa fee payments (student visa MCCs)
- Foreign exchange purchase (study-related amounts)
- Co-applicant (parent) querying education products in copilot
