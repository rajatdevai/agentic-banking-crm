**Document Type:** Product Catalog  
**Version:** 2024-Q4

## Overview

Our Education Loan for Abroad Studies finances higher education at internationally recognised universities. The loan covers tuition fees, accommodation, travel, books, and living expenses. Integration with our Forex Card product enables direct disbursement to overseas institutions and monthly living allowances.

---

## Supported Countries and Institutions

### Countries Covered
- United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand
- Germany, France, Netherlands, Sweden, Denmark (Schengen zone)
- Singapore, UAE (for specific MBA programs)
- Ireland, Japan, South Korea

### Institution Requirements
- Must appear in QS World University Rankings (top 500) OR Times Higher Education Rankings (top 500)
- Accreditation by the country's official higher education body is mandatory
- Community colleges and online-only programs are not eligible
- Pathway/foundation programs: eligible only if linked to a ranking institution

## Interest Rates

Rate is linked to LIBOR (USD LIBOR or equivalent SOFR benchmark) + spread for foreign currency disbursements, or MCLR + spread for INR disbursements:

| Loan Amount | Collateral Status | Interest Rate (p.a.) |
|------------|------------------|---------------------|
| Up to ₹7,50,000 | No collateral required | MCLR + 2.50% (≈ 12.00%) |
| ₹7,50,001 to ₹25,00,000 | Third-party guarantee or partial collateral | MCLR + 2.00% (≈ 11.50%) |
| Above ₹25,00,000 | Immovable property collateral mandatory | MCLR + 1.50% (≈ 11.00%) |

**Interest concession:** 0.50% reduction for female students. 0.25% additional concession for students admitted to IIT/IIM/AIIMS (India premium institution to foreign tier-1 transfer).

## Forex Card Integration

A key differentiator of our education loan is seamless disbursement through our Forex Multi-Currency Card:

- **Pre-loaded for first semester:** First semester fees disbursed directly to university account; balance loaded to Forex Card for living expenses
- **Monthly top-up:** Monthly allowance pre-approved at loan sanction — customer requests top-up digitally; loaded within 24 hours
- **Currencies supported:** USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, CAD, SGD, JPY, CHF, SEK
- **Rate transparency:** Exchange rate locked at loan disbursement date for 90-day windows (reduces forex risk for tuition payments)
- **Emergency limit:** One-time emergency credit of USD 2,000 equivalent available via 24×7 helpline

---

## Disbursement Process

1. **Sanction:** Letter issued within 7 working days of complete document submission
2. **University fee:** Paid directly via SWIFT transfer (no cash handling)
3. **Living expenses:** Credited to Forex Card monthly per approved budget
4. **Second/subsequent years:** Annual disbursement on production of progression certificate

---

## Documents Required

1. Confirmed admission letter from the overseas university
2. Identity proof (Passport mandatory — must be valid for entire course duration)
3. Co-applicant (parent/guardian): Income proof + property documents
4. Academic mark sheets (10th, 12th, graduation)
5. GRE/GMAT/IELTS/TOEFL scores (as applicable)
6. Estimated cost of study from university (I-20 or equivalent)
7. Visa approval (required only before final disbursement — sanction can proceed without)
