# Home Loan Guide — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Product Catalog | Version: 3.0 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Overview
Home loans finance purchase, construction, or renovation of residential property.
Maximum LTV (Loan-to-Value): 90% for loans up to ₹30 lakh; 80% up to ₹75 lakh; 75% above ₹75 lakh.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: 21–70 years (at loan maturity)
- **Minimum income**: ₹40,000/month net (salaried) or ₹5 lakh p.a. ITR (self-employed)
- **CIBIL score**: 700+ preferred; 650–699 considered with higher rate
- **FOIR**: Combined EMIs ≤ 55% of gross monthly income

## Loan Parameters
| Parameter | Range |
|-----------|-------|
| Loan amount | ₹5 lakh – ₹10 crore |
| Tenure | Up to 30 years |
| Interest rate | 8.5% – 11% p.a. (floating / fixed options) |
| Processing fee | 0.5% of loan amount |

## Conversion Signals
- Real estate MCC transactions (property registration, stamp duty payments)
- Searches for property-related terms in copilot
- Large lump-sum debit (possibly earnest money payment)
- Locality change detected (possible relocation)
