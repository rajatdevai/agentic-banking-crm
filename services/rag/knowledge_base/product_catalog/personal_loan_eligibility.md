# Personal Loan Eligibility — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Product Catalog | Version: 2.1 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Overview
Personal loans are unsecured credit products for salaried and self-employed individuals.
No collateral required. Funds disbursed within 24–72 hours of approval.

## Eligibility Criteria

### Salaried Individuals
- **Age**: 21–60 years at loan maturity
- **Minimum salary**: ₹25,000/month (net take-home)
- **Employment tenure**: Minimum 6 months at current employer; total work experience ≥ 2 years
- **CIBIL score**: 700 and above (preferred: 750+)
- **Employer type**: Listed company, MNC, government, PSU, or approved private employer
- **Existing EMI obligation**: Total EMIs (including proposed) ≤ 50% of net monthly income (FOIR)

### Self-Employed / Business Owners
- **Age**: 25–65 years
- **Business vintage**: Minimum 2 years profitable operations
- **ITR**: Last 2 years filed; minimum net annual income ₹3,00,000
- **CIBIL score**: 700 and above
- **GST registration**: Required for businesses with turnover > ₹40 lakh

## Loan Parameters
| Parameter | Range |
|-----------|-------|
| Loan amount | ₹50,000 – ₹40,00,000 |
| Tenure | 12 – 60 months |
| Interest rate | 10.5% – 18% p.a. (reducing balance) |
| Processing fee | 1–2% of loan amount + GST |
| Prepayment charges | NIL after 12 EMIs |

## Disqualifying Conditions
- Active personal loan overdue > 30 days
- 3+ hard enquiries in last 6 months
- Employer on bank's negative list
- CIBIL score < 650 (auto-decline)
- Currently under debt restructuring or settlement

## Priority Conversion Signals (for RM use)
- Customer has NO existing personal loan (high cross-sell opportunity)
- Wedding / home renovation spending detected in transactions
- Salary credit increased > 20% in last 3 months (promotion signal)
- Balance drops sharply despite stable income (large expense approaching)
