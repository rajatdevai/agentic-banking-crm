## Overview

A Personal Loan from RM Copilot Bank is an unsecured term loan that gives customers immediate liquidity without requiring collateral. It is ideal for planned life events — weddings, home renovation, travel, education fees — and unplanned needs such as medical emergencies.

---

## Eligibility Criteria

### Core Requirements (All Applicants)

| Parameter | Minimum Requirement |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Age | 21 years to 58 years (at loan maturity) |
| Employment Type | Salaried or Self-employed (professionally qualified) |
| Minimum Net Monthly Salary | ₹25,000 per month |
| Minimum Salary History | 6 continuous months with the same employer |
| CIBIL Score | 700 and above |
| Debt-to-Income Ratio | Below 50% (including proposed EMI) |
| KYC Status | Complete KYC mandatory before disbursement |
| Existing Relationship | Minimum 3 months account history preferred |

### Loan Parameters

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Minimum Loan Amount | ₹50,000 |
| Maximum Loan Amount | 40× net monthly salary (subject to credit policy cap) |
| Absolute Maximum | ₹40,00,000 |
| Tenure | 12 months to 60 months |
| Repayment Mode | EMI via NACH/auto-debit |
| Pre-closure | Allowed after 12 months; nil penalty for existing savings account holders |
| Part-payment | Allowed once per year (minimum 3 EMIs as lump sum) |

### Interest Rate Band

Interest rate is risk-based and depends on CIBIL score, employment type, and relationship vintage:

| CIBIL Band | Interest Rate (p.a.) | Processing Fee |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------|
| 750 and above | 10.50% – 11.50% | 1.00% of loan amount |
| 700 – 749 | 12.00% – 13.50% | 1.50% of loan amount |
| 650 – 699 | 14.00% – 16.00% | 2.00% of loan amount |
| Below 650 | Not eligible for unsecured personal loan | — |

> **Note:** Existing home loan or FD customers with salary credit to our bank get an additional 0.25% rate concession.

## Persona-Specific Processing Notes

### IT Professionals (Young IT Professional persona)

- **Fast-track processing:** Pre-approved personal loan offer generated within 4 business hours for IT company employees (approved employer list on intranet)
- **Digital journey:** Full end-to-end digital application — no branch visit required
- **Employer verification:** Automated salary slip parsing via API integration with payroll platforms (ADP, Darwinbox, Keka)
- **Special offer:** EMI holiday of 1 month available on loan amounts above ₹5,00,000

### Doctors and Medical Professionals

- **Preferential rate:** Flat 0.50% rate reduction for registered MBBS/MD practitioners (MCI registration required)
- **Higher DTI tolerance:** DTI up to 60% allowed for doctors with 5+ years of practice (practice certificate required)
- **Loan cap enhancement:** Up to ₹75,00,000 for specialist doctors with private practice income
- **Collateral option:** Pledge of medical equipment as partial collateral reduces rate further

### Lawyers and Legal Professionals

- **Preferential rate:** Flat 0.50% rate reduction for advocates enrolled with Bar Council
- **Income calculation:** Average of last 3 years' ITR used for income assessment (not just salary)
- **Higher loan cap:** Up to ₹50,00,000 against demonstrated practice income

### Startup Founders and Business Owners

- **Income assessment:** Average monthly net income from latest 2 years' ITR accepted
- **Seasonal income:** Variable income smoothed over 12 months
- **Maximum loan:** 30× average monthly income (lower than salaried cap due to variability)
- **Bank statement:** 12 months primary account statements mandatory

## Documents Required

### Salaried Applicants
1. Identity proof (Aadhaar / Passport)
2. Last 3 months' salary slips
3. Last 6 months' bank statements (salary account)
4. Form 16 (last 2 years)
5. Employment confirmation letter (for tenures under 1 year)

### Self-Employed / Professionals
1. Identity proof
2. Last 2 years' ITR with computation sheet
3. Last 12 months' bank statements
4. Professional registration certificate (for doctors, lawyers, CA)
5. GST returns (last 4 quarters, if applicable)

---

## Disbursement

- Funds credited to the applicant's savings account within **2 working days** of document completion
- For pre-approved customers: same-day disbursement
- Part-disbursement not available for personal loans (full amount disbursed at once)

## Key Selling Points for RM

1. **No collateral required** — ideal for customers who do not want to pledge assets
2. **Flexible tenure** — customer can choose 12 to 60 months based on affordability
3. **Pre-closure flexibility** — customers feel control over their debt
4. **Consolidation use case** — can help customers consolidate higher-interest credit card debt
5. **Life event anchoring** — frame the loan around the event (wedding, travel) not the necessity

---

## RM Conversation Guidance

- **Opening:** Ask about the upcoming event before mentioning loan — understand the need first
- **Anchor on experience:** "This loan means you can plan your wedding without compromising on anything that matters."
- **EMI calculation:** Always show EMI first — monthly outflow anchoring works better than total interest
- **Rate comparison:** Compare against credit card interest (typically 36–42% p.a.) when relevant
- **Pre-closure pitch:** Emphasise zero penalty for existing customers — reduces psychological lock-in
