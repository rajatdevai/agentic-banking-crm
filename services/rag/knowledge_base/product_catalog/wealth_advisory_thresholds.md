# Wealth Advisory & Investment Products — AUM Tiers and Thresholds

**Document Type:** Product Catalog  
**Version:** 2024-Q4  
**Effective Date:** 01-Oct-2024

---

## Overview

Our Wealth Advisory division offers tiered investment and portfolio management services based on Assets Under Management (AUM). Each tier provides differentiated service levels, product access, and relationship depth. Upgrading customers through tiers is a key retention and revenue strategy for RMs.

---

## Service Tier Structure

### Tier 1 — Mass Affluent (AUM ₹5 Lakhs to ₹10 Lakhs)

**Target Persona:** Young IT professionals, junior corporate executives, first-time investors

**Products Available:**
- Mutual Fund SIP recommendations (curated basket of 3–5 funds)
- Fixed Deposits with sweep facility
- Insurance bundling (term + health)
- Digital portfolio tracking dashboard
- Quarterly portfolio review with RM

**Advisory Model:** Self-directed with RM nudges. Recommendations generated by algorithm; RM validates and personalises.

**Key Metrics for RM:**
- Target: Grow AUM from ₹5L to ₹10L within 18 months through SIP escalations
- Revenue: Trail commission on MF AUM (approximately 0.5% per annum)

---

### Tier 2 — Wealth Advisory (AUM ₹10 Lakhs to ₹50 Lakhs)

**Target Persona:** Senior IT professionals, doctors, lawyers, business owners with established cash flows

**Products Available:**
- All Tier 1 products
- Direct Mutual Fund access (lower expense ratio — saves 0.5–1% vs regular plans)
- PMS (Portfolio Management Service) eligibility from ₹50L threshold preview
- Structured Products (capital protected notes, market-linked debentures)
- NPS (National Pension System) contribution advisory
- Sovereign Gold Bonds (SGB) — tax-efficient gold exposure
- Tax optimisation review (ELSS, HRA structuring, NPS deduction)

**Advisory Model:** Dedicated Wealth Relationship Manager (WRM) assigned. Monthly touchpoint. Annual investment plan prepared.

**Key Metrics for RM:**
- Target: Prevent wealth migration (AUM outflow to competitors)
- Revenue: Advisory fee 0.75% p.a. on assets above ₹10L threshold
- Upsell trigger: Any single SIP above ₹50,000/month signals wealth migration risk — escalate to senior WRM

---

### Tier 3 — Private Banking (AUM above ₹50 Lakhs)

**Target Persona:** HNI, startup founders post-funding/exit, senior corporate professionals, NRI families

**Products Available:**
- All Tier 2 products
- Portfolio Management Service (discretionary and advisory)
- Alternative Investment Funds (AIF) — Category I, II, III
- Real Estate Investment Trusts (REIT) and InvITs
- Unlisted equity and pre-IPO opportunities
- Family office services (estate planning, succession advisory)
- International investment advisory (GIFT City, LRS investments)
- Concierge banking (dedicated phone line, home banking, airport lounge access)
- Priority credit underwriting for business loans

**Advisory Model:** Senior Private Banker assigned. Minimum 2 face-to-face meetings per year (home visits for AUM above ₹1 Crore). Investment Policy Statement (IPS) prepared.

**Key Metrics for RM:**
- Revenue: Brokerage, advisory fees, cross-sell of insurance and structured products
- Escalation: Any customer approaching ₹50L AUM should be proactively introduced to Private Banking team

## SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) Recommendations by Persona

### Equity-Debt Allocation by Age Band

| Age Band | Recommended Equity Allocation | Recommended Debt Allocation |
|----------|------------------------------|----------------------------|
| Under 30 | 80–90% | 10–20% |
| 30–40 | 65–75% | 25–35% |
| 40–50 | 50–60% | 40–50% |
| 50–60 | 30–45% | 55–70% |

### Recommended SIP Basket (Tier 1 — Mass Affluent, Age Under 35)

1. **Large Cap Index Fund** (Nifty 50 Index) — 35% allocation — core stable exposure
2. **Flexi Cap Fund** — 30% allocation — active management for alpha generation
3. **Mid Cap Fund** — 20% allocation — growth potential with managed risk
4. **Debt Short Duration Fund** — 15% allocation — liquidity buffer and stability

### Recommended SIP Basket (Tier 2 — Wealth, Age 35–50)

1. **Large Cap Fund** — 30%
2. **Multi-Asset Allocation Fund** — 25% (equity + debt + gold)
3. **International Fund** (US/Global) — 15% — geographic diversification
4. **Corporate Bond Fund** — 20%
5. **Sovereign Gold Bonds** — 10% — inflation hedge, tax-efficient

## Wealth Migration Risk Signals (RM Action Required)

The following signals indicate a customer may be moving wealth to another institution:

- Large one-time debit (above ₹5 lakhs) to an unknown third-party account
- Decline in SIP amount or SIP cancellation
- Increase in cash withdrawals above normal pattern
- New credit card from a competitor bank (visible in credit bureau)
- Reduction in FD renewal despite maturity notifications
- Customer asking for portfolio summary (may be preparing to exit)

**RM Response:** Contact within 24 hours. Do not lead with product — ask about their financial goals. Understand if competitor offered a better rate and match/escalate.

## Tax Efficiency Notes

- **ELSS Mutual Funds:** ₹1.5 lakh deduction under 80C with 3-year lock-in
- **NPS Tier 1:** Additional ₹50,000 deduction under 80CCD(1B) beyond 80C limit
- **Sovereign Gold Bonds:** Capital gains tax exempt on maturity (8-year tenure)
- **Debt Funds (post-2023):** Taxed as per income slab (no LTCG indexation benefit after Finance Act 2023)
- **ULIP:** Tax-free maturity for premium below ₹2.5 lakh/year
