# Wealth Advisory Thresholds — RM Copilot Knowledge Base
## Source: Product Catalog | Version: 2.0 | Effective: 2025-01-01

## Wealth Segment Definitions

### Mass Retail
- Total Assets Under Management (AUM): < ₹10 lakh
- Products: Savings account, FD, RD, basic mutual funds
- RM interaction: Digital-first; human RM on request

### Affluent
- AUM: ₹10 lakh – ₹50 lakh
- Products: All retail + curated mutual funds, SIPs, term insurance, credit cards (Signature tier)
- RM interaction: Assigned RM; quarterly review calls

### High Net Worth Individual (HNI)
- AUM: ₹50 lakh – ₹5 crore
- Products: All affluent + PMS (Portfolio Management Services), structured products, forex advisory
- RM interaction: Dedicated Private Banking RM; monthly reviews
- **Outreach tone**: Lead with wealth growth and capital preservation, NOT with loan products

### Ultra HNI / Private Banking
- AUM: > ₹5 crore
- Products: All HNI + alternative investments (AIFs), estate planning, family office services
- RM interaction: Senior Private Banker; personalised concierge service

## Upgrade Triggers (flag for RM)
- Balance + investments crosses segment threshold — recommend wealth advisory
- Sudden large credit (business sale, property sale, inheritance) — immediate HNI review
- Customer enquires about PMS or structured products — escalate to Private Banking team

## Key Rule for Outreach Generation
When persona_type = HNI or ULTRA_HNI:
  - Never open with loan product pitches
  - Lead with portfolio review, market insights, or wealth protection
  - Loan products are secondary conversation, only if customer initiates
